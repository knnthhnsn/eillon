Fragrance care

How to wear, store, and live with an oil-rich EILLON parfum — composed for closeness, not volume.

Application

EILLON parfums are oil-rich and designed to sit on warm skin. A single spray is often enough; the accord unfolds over hours rather than announcing itself across a room.

  1. Pulse — wrist, collarbone, or behind the ear. Do not rub; heat alone opens the formula.
  2. Cloth — a light mist on scarf, coat lining, or hair ends carries a quieter trail through the day.
  3. Return — one refresh before evening; base notes deepen on skin that has warmed the parfum all day.

Storage

  • Keep bottles upright, away from direct sun and bathroom steam.
  • Store between 15–22°C; avoid rapid temperature swings that can stress natural materials.
  • Keep the cap closed when not in use — the matte flacon is designed for long keep, not daily display in heat.
  • Discovery samples are best used within six months of opening; full bottles within two to three years of purchase.

Layering

EILLON chapters are composed to stand alone. If you layer, pair complementary temperatures rather than competing families:

  • Beles + Asmara — fruit and mineral rain; airy daytime depth.
  • Massawa + Beles — citrus sun over cactus water; warm coastal afternoon.
  • Ritual beneath any chapter — a resin base worn under a lighter spray for evening.

Apply the richer accord first, then the lighter chapter on top after a minute.
