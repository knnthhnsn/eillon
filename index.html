The Maison Palette

The scent worlds of
EILLON.

Each EILLON fragrance begins as a memory of place: desert fruit, rain on stone, Red Sea citrus, sacred resin, café air, and warm skin.

Desert Fruit

Prickly pear, cactus water, pear skin, hibiscus, and mineral air.

Rain on Stone

Petrichor, humid air, espresso, cardamom, jasmine, and amber.

Red Sea Citrus

Orange, papaya, sea air, salt skin, and sun-warmed coastal stone.

Sacred Resin

The Object

A bottle cut from
a single block.

  1. — 01

    The Cap

    A tall rectangular stopper in brushed silver — proportioned to the square and weighted to close without fuss.

  2. — 02

    The Block

    An opaque square flacon in matte glass, corners softly rounded — flat-faced, architectural, without band or label.

  3. — 03

    The Emblem

    A winged leopard stippled at the base in fine line, draped in classical cloth — the bottle's only figurative gesture.

  4. — 04

    The Lettering

    EILLON, Beles, and Fico d'India set in quiet serif above the emblem, pressed directly into the face.

Atmosphere

Sun on stone. Air warm.

Memory held
in light.

EILLON is interpreted as atmosphere: desert fruit at high noon, rain on warm pavement, Red Sea air at the coast, and sacred smoke drifting through dry evening air.

Wear

Worn close
to warmth.

01

Pulse

Apply once to wrist or collarbone, then let the accord open without rubbing.

02

Cloth

Mist lightly over a scarf or lining to carry a close-wearing trail through the day.

03

Return

Refresh with one spray before evening; the heart notes deepen rather than becoming louder.
