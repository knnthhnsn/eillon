The House of Memory

A maisoncomposedfrom memory.

EILLON composes intimate, oil-rich parfums from Afro-Mediterranean memory — Red Sea air, desert fruit, olive grove, rain on stone, café spices, sacred resins, and warm skin, hand-finished in Copenhagen. Each fragrance is a chapter: Beles begins with prickly pear and cactus water; Oliva turns toward sun-warmed olive leaf; Asmara follows rain on old city stone; Massawa turns toward the Red Sea coast.

The Scent Atlas

The scent worlds
of EILLON.

Each fragrance begins as a memory of place — desert fruit, olive grove, rain on stone, Red Sea citrus, wet earth, and sacred resin.

  • 01 — N 36° Tunisia

    Desert Fruit

    Prickly pear, cactus water, pear skin, hibiscus, and mineral air.
  • 02 — N 37° Crete

    Olive Grove

    Olive leaf, bitter fruit, golden oil, thyme, and warm grove stone.
  • 03 — N 15° Asmara · Eritrea

    Rain on Stone

    Petrichor, humid air, espresso, cardamom, jasmine, and amber.
  • 04 — N 15° Massawa

    Red Sea Citrus

    Orange, papaya, sea air, salt skin, and sun-warmed coastal stone.
  • 05 — N 55° Copenhagen

    Wet Earth

    Geosmin, ozone, wet clay, crushed stems, and mineral musk on limestone.
  • 06 — N 17° Dhofar

    Sacred Resin

    Frankincense, myrrh, candle smoke, labdanum, woods, and warm skin.

The Object

A bottle cut from a single block.

  1. 01

    The Cap

    A tall rectangular stopper in brushed silver — proportioned to the square and weighted to close without fuss.

  2. 02

    The Block

    An opaque square flacon in matte glass, corners softly rounded — flat-faced, architectural, without band or label.

  1. 03

    The Emblem

    A winged leopard stippled at the base in fine line, draped in classical cloth — the bottle's only figurative gesture.

  2. 04

    The Lettering

    EILLON, Beles, and Fico d'India set in quiet serif above the emblem, pressed into the face.

The Wear

Worn close to warmth.

01

Pulse

Apply once to wrist or collarbone, then let the accord open on warm skin — without rubbing.

02

Cloth

Mist lightly over a scarf or lining to carry a close-wearing trail through the day.

03

Return

Refresh with one spray before evening; the heart notes deepen rather than becoming louder.

    What is EILLON?

    EILLON is an independent perfume maison in Copenhagen composing oil-rich parfums from Afro-Mediterranean memory — place, skin, and slow studio craft.

    EILLON is a niche fragrance house—not a mass-market label. Chapters are filed like correspondence: composed in Copenhagen, documented in the archive, and released in deliberate windows rather than constant retail rotation.
