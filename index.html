Chapter I — Inspiration

A study in rain
stone & quiet earth.

Named for Asmara, Eritrea's capital city, this fragrance was composed around the first minutes after rainfall — when warm pavement exhales, when wet stone darkens, and when the air turns mineral and green.

Built around a clear petrichor accord, Asmara holds damp earth, rainwater, and softened moss in an architectural calm. It is intimate. Considered. Unhurried — a quiet luxury shaped by place, weather, and memory.

Chapter II — Composition

FICO D'INDIA
architecture.

Three movements, drawn carefully into one another — designed to ripen on the skin as sun warms the fruit on the pad.

01 Top

Prickly Pear · Bergamot · Green Fig

A bright opening — sliced pear, cool citrus, and the clean green of fig leaf. Juicy, lifted, and immediately alive.

02 Heart

Cactus Flower · Pear Blossom · Soft Spice

The Beles accord — floral, warm, and quietly sunlit. Ripe fruit held with polished restraint.

03 Base

Amber · Vetiver · Clean Musk

A grounded close — dry warmth, soft resin, and clean skin in late afternoon. The fragrance settles into a calm, golden trace.

Accord profile

Beles · Fico d'India, built as a quiet balance of ripe fruit, desert green, soft bloom, and warm skin.

Chapter III — The Object

A bottle cut from
a single block.

  1. — 01

    The Cap

    A tall rectangular stopper in brushed silver — proportioned to the square and weighted to close without fuss.

  2. — 02

    The Block

    An opaque square flacon in matte glass, corners softly rounded — flat-faced, architectural, without band or label.

  3. — 03

    The Emblem

    A winged leopard stippled at the base in fine line, draped in classical cloth — the bottle's only figurative gesture.

  4. — 04

    The Lettering

    EILLON, Beles, and Fico d'India set in quiet serif above the emblem, pressed directly into the face.

Chapter IV — Atmosphere

Sun on cactus. Air warm.

Ripe fruit at
high noon.

Beles · Fico d'India is interpreted as atmosphere: a sunlit terrace, prickly pear split open, blossom on warm stone, and the slow drift of amber through dry afternoon air.

Chapter V — Ritual

Worn close
to warmth.

01

Pulse

02

Cloth

03

Return

Refresh with one spray before evening; the amber notes deepen rather than becoming louder.

Ingredients

Parfum (perfume oil concentrate), alcohol denat., aqua, benzyl salicylate, limonene, linalool, coumarin, citral. Carrier alcohol is present as in all fine fragrance; the composition is built around a high perfume-oil ratio, not a diluted eau de toilette.

FAQ
Is Beles oil-based?
Yes — Beles · Fico d'India is an oil-rich parfum with a high perfume-oil concentration, composed for depth on skin rather than as a diluted alcohol-forward eau de toilette.
What is Fico d'India?
Italian for prickly pear — the sun-warmed fruit accord at the heart of Beles. Read more in the journal →
Where is EILLON composed?
In Copenhagen, Denmark — hand-finished at EILLON studio.
How does the waitlist work?
Join with your email and preferred size. When the next release opens, you receive a private purchase link before the public boutique.
How does the sample credit work?
The € 28 discovery sample is credited toward any 50 ml or 100 ml bottle ordered within 30 days.
How should I test an oil-based parfum?
Apply once to skin and wear for at least three hours without rubbing; the floral heart opens as the fruit accord settles.
How should I store it?
Keep the bottle away from direct sunlight, heat, and humid shelves so the fruit accord stays bright.
