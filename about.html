About EILLON

An independent perfume house in Copenhagen — composing intimate, genderless parfums from Afro-Mediterranean memory: Red Sea air, desert fruit, rain on stone, and warm skin.

Origin

EILLON began in Copenhagen as a studio question: can a perfume hold the feeling of a place after you have left it? The name carries Red Sea memory — coastlines between Africa and the Mediterranean, desert fruit at noon, rain on stone streets, and skin warmed by sun and wind. The maison does not chase trends; it composes chapters slowly, each tied to a specific atmosphere.

Place, memory & skin

EILLON began as a question of atmosphere: how does a place stay on the skin after you leave it? The maison answers with chapters — each fragrance a memory of Red Sea air, desert fruit at high noon, rain on old city stone, café spice, sacred resin, and warm skin held close.

Beles · Fico d'India opened the maison — prickly pear and cactus water composed as an oil-rich parfum. Asmara follows rain on stone. Massawa turns toward the coast. Ritual studies frankincense and myrrh in the lab. Beles awaits its next release; other chapters are in development or held as studio studies.

The studio in Copenhagen

EILLON is composed and hand-finished in Copenhagen. The studio works in small batches, with formulas developed in-house and objects designed as architecture — square matte glass, brushed silver, quiet lettering pressed into the face.

Private fragrance appointments are held Thursday to Saturday, 12–18. To request a visit, write to care@eillon.maison.
