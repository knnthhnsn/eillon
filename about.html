About EILLON

An independent perfume house in Copenhagen — composing intimate, genderless parfums from Afro-Mediterranean memory.

Place, memory & skin

EILLON began as a question of atmosphere: how does a place stay on the skin after you leave it? The maison answers with chapters — each fragrance a memory of Red Sea air, desert fruit at high noon, rain on old city stone, café spice, sacred resin, and warm skin held close.

Beles · Fico d'India is the first release — prickly pear and cactus water composed as an oil-rich parfum. Asmara follows rain on stone. Massawa turns toward the coast. Ritual studies frankincense and myrrh in the lab. Nothing is rushed; each chapter is refined before it is offered.

The studio in Copenhagen

EILLON is composed and hand-finished in Copenhagen. The studio works in small batches, with formulas developed in-house and objects designed as architecture — square matte glass, brushed silver, quiet lettering pressed into the face.

Private fragrance appointments are held Thursday to Saturday, 12–18. To request a visit, write to care@eillon.maison.

Ethos

  • Intimate parfums with a high perfume-oil load — worn close, unfolding slowly.
  • Genderless compositions built from place and memory, not trend.
  • Transparent release windows: waitlist and letters before public boutique openings.
  • Objects and formulas developed together — bottle, cap, and accord as one thought.
