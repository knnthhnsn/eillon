Shipping & returns

How EILLON orders travel from the Copenhagen studio to you — and how returns work when a bottle is not quite right.

Founder preorder dispatch

The Beles founder sample preorder dispatches after the first founder batch is ready and has passed final inspection. The wording displayed on the preorder page at payment is the applicable expected window; no fixed release date is guaranteed unless a dated window is published there.

The €30 bottle reservation is a deposit, not a shipped item and not full payment for a 50 ml or 100 ml bottle. A private purchase window, final balance, and bottle dispatch terms are sent separately. Sample preorders may be cancelled for a refund before dispatch. Bottle deposits are refundable before shipment; if a deposit has already been applied to a final bottle order, cancellation before dispatch includes that deposit amount.

Shipping

When bottles are offered for sale, complimentary delivery applies to Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Germany, France, the United Kingdom, and the United States unless otherwise stated in your order confirmation. Each flacon is hand-wrapped in archival cream paper and packed to protect the cap, emblem, and glass faces in transit.

  • Denmark & EU — typically 2–5 business days after dispatch.
  • United Kingdom, US & rest of world — typically 5–12 business days after dispatch.
  • Tracking — provided by email when your order leaves the studio.
  • Perfume limitations — carrier and dangerous-goods rules can restrict particular routes. If a paid preorder route cannot be served, EILLON cancels and refunds that preorder.

Dated dispatch windows are confirmed in writing only when they can be supported. Until then, the founder sample uses the readiness-and-inspection window published on the preorder page and does not imply a fixed date.

Returns

Unopened orders may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund of the product price, unless your order confirmation states otherwise. To begin a return, email care@eillon.maison with your order reference.

  • Bottles must be unused, with seal intact and minimal outer wear from transit.
  • Discovery samples opened for wear are not returnable once the seal is broken.
  • Engraved or made-to-order pieces are final sale unless faulty.
  • Return shipping costs are covered by EILLON for EU orders; other regions are handled case by case.

This voluntary return policy does not limit mandatory consumer cancellation, conformity, or guarantee rights.

Sample credit

The €28 Beles founder sample payment is credited toward one 50 ml or 100 ml bottle purchased within 30 days after sample dispatch. Credit applies once per customer for the founder release and cannot be combined with other promotions unless noted.

Damaged or missing items

If a bottle arrives damaged or an item is missing, contact us within 48 hours with photographs of the packaging and flacon. We will replace or refund at no additional cost.

Questions

For shipping or return enquiries: care@eillon.maison. Full legal terms: Terms.

How does the Beles founder preorder work?

The founder file has two paid paths when checkout is open: a €28 preorder for one 2 ml sample, or a €30 refundable deposit for a private 50 ml or 100 ml purchase window.

The deposit is credited to the later bottle purchase. No full 50 ml or 100 ml bottle payment is taken on the preorder page, and the page shows its live status before any Stripe redirect.
