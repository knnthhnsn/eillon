## Conversion paths

### Beles restock (primary)
`Search/social ā†’ /prickly-pear-parfum or /journal/* ā†’ /beles ā†’ trust microcopy ā†’ #waitlist form ā†’ POST /api/waitlist`  
Trust block: `.shop__restock-trust` (one email, no charge, size-as-interest, privacy link) ā€” EXP-005  
Events: `restock_form_started`, `restock_form_submitted`, `restock_form_error`  
Product slug: `beles`

### The Letter (newsletter)
`Footer / about / journal / store ā†’ form (product_slug=all, source=newsletter|footer) ā†’ /api/waitlist`  
Success copy in `script.js` (`waitlistMessages.all`)

### Private Copenhagen appointment
`mailto:care@eillon.maison` with subject variants  
Event: `studio_appointment_click` (homepage footer, nav footer)  
No booking form ā€” email handoff

### Chapter waitlists (secondary)
`/asmara`, `/massawa`, `/ritual` ā†’ `#waitlist` ā†’ API

### Dynamic store cards
`script.js` renders mini-waitlist on eligible product cards
