# Campaign: Prickly pear, but not candy

**EXP-ID:** EXP-006  
**Loop:** social_to_letter

**Date:** 2026-06-29  
**Status:** draft — do not publish off-repo without human review

## Hypothesis

If we publish quiet social hooks that position prickly pear as mineral, airy, and skin-close—not candy-sweet—then visual-discovery audiences on Pinterest, Instagram, and TikTok will click through to Beles or The Letter, because the copy matches niche buyer language and rejects generic fruit-perfume expectations.

## Campaign angle

**Brand fit:** 5/5 — sensory contrast is core EILLON positioning for Beles · Fico d'India.

Most prickly-pear fragrance content skews sugary or tropical. Beles reads green-pink, watery, sun-warm, and close to skin: pear skin, cactus water, hibiscus, mineral air, warm stone. The campaign reframes the note as architecture, not dessert.
