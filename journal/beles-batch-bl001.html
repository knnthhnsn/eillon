Safety & labelling

Beles · Fico d'India was reviewed against IFRA Category 4 limits for fine fragrance intended for skin wear. Label allergens for BL-001 match the chapter page declaration: benzyl salicylate, limonene, linalool, coumarin, citral. Conformity documentation is available on request before public commerce opens.

Wear evaluation linked to this batch

Six studio wear sessions (eight-hour intervals, standard dose on inner forearm) informed the longevity language on /beles. Results are published as observed wear — not a guarantee for every skin type. Methodology: craftsmanship · wear testing.

What does IFRA Category 4 mean in plain language?

IFRA Category 4 is the fine-fragrance-on-skin category—limits how much of each raw material can safely appear in a parfum worn on pulse points.

Beles was reviewed against Category 4 limits; allergen declarations on the chapter page match batch BL-001 labelling notes.
