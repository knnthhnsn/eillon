What is a skin scent?

EILLON studio · 29 June 2026

A skin scent is parfum composed to stay close to the body — readable on pulse points and collarbone rather than filling a room. If you search for a quiet niche perfume that unfolds on skin over hours, this is the wear intent behind the phrase.

Close-wearing, not invisible

Skin scent does not mean weak. Projection and longevity are separate choices. A close-wearing parfum can last through an evening while staying intimate — you, and someone within arm's reach. Oil-rich formulas often read this way because they reward patience: the heart opens as the top accord dries down.

How it differs from loud projection

Room-filling sprays chase distance. Skin scents chase warmth — wrist, collarbone, the line of a scarf. EILLON composes for the second read: mineral musk and fruit placed against air, not sugar stacked for sillage. Fragrance care guide →
