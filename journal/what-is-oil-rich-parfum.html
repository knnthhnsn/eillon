What is oil-rich parfum?

EILLON studio · 29 June 2026

Oil-rich parfum is a fragrance built with a high load of perfume oils in the composition — higher concentration than eau de toilette or many eau de parfums. At EILLON we compose in this category because it rewards patience: the opening, heart, and dry-down read in sequence on warm skin, close to the body. Beles · Fico d'India is our current chapter in oil-rich parfum.

Parfum vs eau de toilette — what changes on skin

Eau de toilette is typically more diluted and alcohol-forward. The first spray can feel bright and immediate, but the accord may fade faster. Oil-rich parfum carries more perfume oils, so fruit, floral, and musk accords have room to ripen. Projection stays nearer skin — a quiet skin scent rather than a room-filling cloud.

Why we compose oil-rich at EILLON

Our chapters — prickly pear, rain on stone, Red Sea citrus — need depth to hold memory without shouting. An oil-rich base lets a fruit accord like Fico d'India open watery and green, then fold into hibiscus and mineral musk over hours. That slow unfold is the point. How to wear and test it

Apply once to wrist or collarbone. Do not rub — friction heats and compresses the opening. Wait. Oil-rich parfums typically show their heart accord after the top notes settle, often within the first three hours on skin. After six hours the dry-down — musk, stone, mineral air — reads most clearly. Fragrance care guide →

What oil-rich parfum is not

  • Not a guarantee of all-day projection on every skin type
  • Not the same as a pure oil perfume with no alcohol carrier — verify formula on each product page
  • Not louder by default — concentration supports depth, not volume
  • Not gendered — EILLON parfums are composed for skin, not a demographic label
