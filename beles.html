Beles • Fico d'India
Prickly pear, mineral air, soft musk.

Beles is a green-pink cactus fruit parfum: watery prickly pear, cactus water, pear skin, hibiscus, soft musk, and sun-warmed mineral air.

  • Oil-rich parfum concentration — close, long-wearing trail
  • Fico d'India accord: prickly pear, cactus water, hibiscus, soft musk
  • Composed in Copenhagen · 2 ml sample, 50 ml, 100 ml
  • Waitlist open — priority access at first release

Composition

Three movements drawn into one another: a bright fruit opening, a warm floral heart, and a grounded mineral drydown. The oil-rich concentration carries the accord slowly — ripening on skin rather than evaporating in the first hour.

Top

Prickly pear · Cactus water · Pear skin

Heart

Hibiscus · Cactus bloom · Green leaves

Base

Soft musk · Mineral air · Warm stone

Why oil-rich parfum

Beles is composed as an oil-rich parfum, designed to unfold slowly and wear close to skin. You wear less. It lasts longer. The fruit accord opens over hours, not minutes.

FAQ

Is Beles oil-based?
Yes. Beles · Fico d'India is an oil-rich parfum with a high perfume-oil concentration — composed for depth and a close-wearing trail on skin.
What is Fico d'India?
Italian for prickly pear. It names the sun-warmed fruit accord at the centre of Beles and appears on the bottle below the fragrance name.
Where is it composed?
In Copenhagen, Denmark, by EILLON studio — hand-finished and presented in archival packaging.
