FAQ
Is Beles an oil-rich parfum?
Yes — Beles · Fico d'India is an oil-rich parfum with a high perfume-oil concentration, composed for depth and a close-wearing trail on skin.
What is Fico d'India?
Italian for prickly pear — the sun-warmed fruit accord at the heart of Beles. Read more in the journal →
Where is EILLON composed?
In Copenhagen, Denmark — hand-finished at EILLON studio.

How should I test an oil-rich parfum?
Apply once to skin and wear for at least three hours without rubbing; the floral heart opens as the fruit accord settles.
How long does Beles last on skin?
Beles is an oil-rich parfum composed to unfold slowly and wear close. In studio wear sessions — six wearers, standard dose, eight-hour intervals — we observe roughly 8–10 hours before the mineral drydown fades to a close trace. Longevity varies with skin, climate, and application; these figures describe our studio experience, not a guarantee. How we evaluate wear →
How should I store it?
Keep the bottle away from direct sunlight, heat, and humid shelves so the fruit accord stays bright.
Composition

FICO D'INDIA
architecture.

Three movements, drawn carefully into one another — designed to ripen on the skin as sun warms the fruit on the pad.

01 Top

Prickly Pear · Cactus Water · Pear Skin

A bright opening — watery prickly pear, cactus water, and pear skin. Juicy, lifted, and immediately alive.

02 Heart

Hibiscus · Cactus Bloom · Green Leaves

The Beles accord — floral, warm, and quietly sunlit. Ripe fruit held with polished restraint.

03 Base

Soft Musk · Mineral Air · Warm Stone

A grounded close — soft musk, mineral air, and warm stone on skin. The fragrance settles into a calm, close-wearing trace.

Why oil-rich parfum

Beles is composed as an oil-rich parfum, designed to unfold slowly and wear close to skin. You wear less. It lasts longer. The fruit accord opens over hours, not minutes.
