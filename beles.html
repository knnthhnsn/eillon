Composition

Three movements drawn into one another: a bright fruit opening (prickly pear, bergamot, green fig), a warm floral heart (cactus flower, pear blossom, soft spice), and a grounded amber drydown (resin, vetiver root, clean musk). The oil base carries the accord slowly — ripening on skin rather than evaporating in the first hour.

Why oil-based parfum

Most mass-market fragrances are diluted for volume — high alcohol, low oil, short life on skin. Beles is composed as a parfum: a higher ratio of precious perfume oils to carrier, built for depth, longevity, and a close trail. You wear less. It lasts longer. The fruit accord unfolds over hours, not minutes.

FAQ

Is Beles oil-based?
Yes. Beles · Fico d'India is an oil-rich parfum with a high perfume-oil concentration — composed for depth and longevity, not as a diluted eau de toilette.
What is Fico d'India?
Italian for prickly pear. It names the sun-warmed fruit accord at the centre of Beles and appears on the bottle below the fragrance name.
Where is it composed?
In Copenhagen, Denmark, by EILLON studio — hand-finished and presented in archival packaging.
