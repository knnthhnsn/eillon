Privacy

EILLON respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you join the Beles waitlist, subscribe to The Letter, or contact the studio — and how we use it.

Data controller

EILLON studio, Copenhagen, Denmark.
Contact: care@eillon.maison

What we collect

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist or newsletter.
  • Size preference — if you select a volume on the boutique waitlist form.
  • Source — whether you signed up via the waitlist or newsletter.
  • Timestamps — when your signup was created or updated.

We do not collect payment details through this website. Purchases, when offered, are confirmed separately by email.

Why we use it

  • To manage the Beles waitlist and send release updates.
  • To send seasonal EILLON letters, if you subscribed.
  • To reply to care, press, and appointment requests.

Legal basis

We process your email on the basis of your consent when you submit a form, and our legitimate interest in operating the studio and responding to enquiries.

Storage & processors

Signups are stored in a secure PostgreSQL database (Neon), hosted in the EU, via our hosting provider Vercel. Email is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their marketing.

Retention

We keep your email until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it, or until it is no longer needed for the purpose it was collected.

Your rights

Under GDPR you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data, or withdraw consent at any time. Email care@eillon.maison. You may also lodge a complaint with the Danish Data Protection Agency (Datatilsynet).
