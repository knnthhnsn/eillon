Privacy

How EILLON handles waitlist, newsletter, and appointment data.

EILLON respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you join the Beles waitlist, subscribe to The Letter, or contact the studio — and how we use it.

Data controller

EILLON studio, Copenhagen, Denmark (CVR 43933485).
Contact: care@eillon.maison

What we collect

  • Email address — when you join a restock list, chapter follow list, or newsletter.
  • Name — optional, when you provide it on a signup form.
  • Product / chapter — which fragrance or list you signed up for (e.g. Beles, Asmara, newsletter).
  • Size preference — if you select a volume on the Beles restock form (interest only, not an order).
  • Form source — whether you signed up via restock, newsletter, store, or another page.
  • UTM parameters — campaign source, medium, and name when present in the URL.
  • Timestamps — when your signup was created or updated.
  • Consent record — that you agreed to the stated purpose, when consent was given, and the privacy notice version in effect (currently 24 June 2026).
  • Notification purpose — restock note, chapter development update, or newsletter.

We do not collect payment details through this website. Purchases, when offered, are confirmed separately by email.

Why we use it

  • To send restock notes and release updates for the chapter you requested.
  • To send seasonal EILLON letters, if you subscribed to The Letter.
  • To send occasional studio notes for chapters in development or lab studies you chose to follow.
  • To reply to care, press, and appointment requests.

Legal basis

We process your email on the basis of your consent when you submit a form for a specific purpose (restock note, chapter follow, or newsletter). Form copy states what you are signing up for. We also rely on our legitimate interest in operating the studio and responding to enquiries.

Storage & processors

Signups are stored in a secure PostgreSQL database (Neon), hosted in the EU. The site is hosted on Vercel. Transactional signup notifications may be sent through Resend. Anonymous usage analytics and real-user performance metrics may be collected through Vercel Analytics and Speed Insights — page paths, device class, campaign parameters, and funnel events only. We do not send names or email addresses to analytics.

Retention & withdrawal

We keep your email until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it, or until it is no longer needed for the purpose it was collected. You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing care@eillon.maison.

Your rights

Under GDPR you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data, or withdraw consent at any time. Email care@eillon.maison. You may also lodge a complaint with the Danish Data Protection Agency (Datatilsynet).
